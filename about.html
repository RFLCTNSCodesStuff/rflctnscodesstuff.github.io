Who are you?


I'm an up-and-coming musician, author, artist, gamer, and photographer with interests in franchises like Touhou, Len'en, Celeste, Pokémon, and more.


Is there anything more we should know about you?


I'm the host of an endogenic system that resulted from my tinkering with various characters; some of them are mine, others aren't. These characters live alongside me within my mind, even though some of those characters come from different worlds than mine.


Additionally, I'm neurodivergent, with a diagnosis of Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) at a young age; as such, I have strong intellect and creativity, which is (presumably) what led to the formation of my original characters and system.
